Snowball vs. avalanche, in plain English

What is the debt snowball method?

Pay minimums on everything; throw every spare dollar at the smallest balance. When it dies, its whole payment rolls into the next smallest. You get wins fast, and wins keep you going.

What is the debt avalanche method?

Same idea, but extra money attacks the highest interest rate first. It's mathematically the cheapest path out of debt.

Which should I pick?

Run your numbers above. If avalanche only saves you a little, most people do better with the snowball's motivation. If it saves you a lot, the math is telling you something. Either way — the strategy you stick with beats the one you abandon.

Assumes payments start next month and interest compounds monthly (APR ÷ 12). Educational tool, not financial advice.

Net worth, in plain English

What counts as an asset?

Anything you could turn into money: checking and savings, brokerage and retirement accounts, your home's market value, vehicles (be honest — use what it would sell for), and anything else of real resale value.

Is a negative net worth bad?

It's normal early on — student loans plus a car loan can outweigh savings for years. Direction beats position: a net worth climbing from −$20k is healthier than one stalled at +$5k.

How often should I check it?

Monthly. Frequent enough to stay motivated, rare enough that market wobbles don't shake you. Payday works great.

Savings goals, in plain English

How long will it take to reach my goal?

Rough math: gap ÷ monthly amount. Real math: your balance also earns interest every month, which shortens the wait — that's what this calculator computes (monthly compounding, APY ÷ 12, contribution added each month).

Does the APY really matter?

On short goals, a little. On multi-year goals in a high-yield savings account, a lot more than people expect. Run it with 0% and again with your real APY — the difference is free money.

What if I can't save much?

Pick the number you can hit every month, even if it's small. Consistency compounds; heroic one-off deposits don't. And when a windfall shows up, throw it in and watch the date jump closer.

Assumes contributions at the start of each month and monthly compounding (APY ÷ 12). Educational tool, not financial advice.
